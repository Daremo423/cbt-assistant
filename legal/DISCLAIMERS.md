## Sarcasm & Context Limitations

⚠️ **AI LIMITATIONS**

This app analyzes transcribed text for cognitive distortions. It cannot detect:
- Sarcasm or irony in tone of voice
- Non-verbal context or body language
- Situational appropriateness
- Cultural or contextual nuances

**You may receive alerts for statements that were:**
- Sarcastic/joking
- Contextually appropriate
- Culturally specific expressions
- Quotations or hypothetical examples

Use your judgment when reviewing flagged content.
